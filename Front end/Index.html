First Aid Guide

  • Burns: Cool the burn under running water for 20 minutes.
  • Choking: Perform back blows and abdominal thrusts.
  • Bleeding: Apply pressure and elevate the wound.
  • CPR: 30 chest compressions, 2 breaths. Repeat.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the limb and apply a splint. Seek medical help.
  • Poisoning: Do not induce vomiting. Call emergency services immediately.
  • Heatstroke: Move to a cool place, apply cold packs, and hydrate.
  • Hypothermia: Warm the person gradually and remove wet clothing.
  • Allergic Reaction: Use an epinephrine auto-injector if available. Seek emergency help.

Daily Wellness Tips

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water.
  • Sleep for 7-8 hours every night.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Take short breaks during work or study sessions.
  • Eat fruits and vegetables regularly.
